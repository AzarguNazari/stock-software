Favendo-Actility Auto Configuration

This tool is required to connect ThingPark to Favendo’s API and to configure the Abeeway devices in order to display the tracking results in Favendo’s Asset Tracking Viewer.

Please select the right ThingPark platform (“Community” or “TPE EU”), enter your credentials from the ThingPark Enterprise and confirm with the “Connect & configure” button.

Your credentials won’t be stored by Favendo and will only be used once for this purpose.
